Waffles

Descripton

These tender and flavorful waffles are perfect for breakfast. They will definetly satisfy your sweet tooth! Recipe made for 8 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 and a half cups warm milk
  • 3/4 cup melted and cooled butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 and a half teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 egg whites
